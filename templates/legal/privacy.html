Privacy Policy

At Osrick, we are committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the confidentiality of the information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or use our services.

Effective Date: July 12, 2025

Welcome to Osrick. Your privacy is important to us. Whether you are visiting our website, requesting a quotation, applying for a position, or contacting our team, we are committed to handling your personal information responsibly and transparently.

By accessing or using our website, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when contacting us or using our services.

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Company Name
  • Job Application Information
  • Messages submitted through our contact forms

We may also automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited
  • Referral source
  • Date and time of your visit

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect helps us provide better services and improve your experience on our website.

We may use your information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and support requests.
  • Provide requested BPO and business services.
  • Process job applications.
  • Communicate regarding quotations or projects.
  • Improve website performance and user experience.
  • Maintain website security and prevent fraudulent activities.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Security

Protecting your information is one of our priorities. We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to help protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Although we strive to use commercially acceptable security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and improve website functionality.

Cookies may be used to:

  • Remember your preferences.
  • Improve website performance.
  • Understand how visitors interact with our website.
  • Enhance security and reliability.

You may disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Please note that some website features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website may include links to third-party websites or services. These external websites operate independently and have their own privacy policies.

Osrick is not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or security of third-party websites. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any external websites they visit.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

7. Your Rights

Depending on applicable laws, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Object to certain processing activities.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for businesses and individuals aged 18 or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so that we can remove the information where appropriate.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, legal requirements, or business operations. Any updates will be posted on this page together with the revised Effective Date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us.
